Our Story

Crafted with fire,
served with soul.

Ember & Oak was born from a simple belief — that great food deserves great atmosphere. Every dish we serve is cooked over live fire, using ingredients sourced from farms within 150 miles of our kitchen.

We are not a restaurant that chases trends. We honour craft — the slow art of cooking over flame, the discipline of a well-rested cut of meat, the patience of a properly reduced sauce.
